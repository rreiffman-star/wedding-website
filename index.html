Travel Notes

Plan for boats, bridges, and a little extra time.

Arriving

Venice Marco Polo Airport is the closest major airport. From there, guests can reach the city by water taxi, Alilaguna ferry, or car transfer to Piazzale Roma.

Staying

Hotel suggestions and room block details will be posted here. Staying near San Marco, Dorsoduro, or the Grand Canal will keep the weekend easy.

Moving Around

Venice is best on foot and by vaporetto. Pack comfortable shoes for bridges and bring a small bag for water-taxi transfers.
